About Malino Passion Fruit Syrup

Malino Passion Fruit Syrup is a family-owned business dedicated to creating premium, all-natural passion fruit products. Our journey began with a commitment to quality and authenticity, rebranding from our family's previous venture to eliminate artificial ingredients and focus on pure, natural flavors.

Our Product

100% Natural Ingredients

  • 85% Premium Passion Fruit - sourced from trusted suppliers
  • 15% Pure Sugar and pH Water - no regular water used
  • No artificial ingredients, colors, or citrus
  • All Natural - promoting authentic tropical flavor

Quality Distinction

Our authentic product maintains the natural passion fruit color, distinguishing it from competitors who use artificial coloring that creates an unnatural orange hue. Our commitment to natural ingredients ensures superior taste and quality.

Freshness Guarantee

Shelf Life: 12 months from production date

Stock Rotation: We maintain 3-month stock cycles, restocking every 3 months to ensure freshness

Our Production Process

Artisanal Manufacturing

Each batch is carefully crafted using our specialized equipment that processes 100kg of passion fruit per production cycle. The entire process takes considerable time to ensure optimal flavor extraction and quality.

Seed Separation Process

We employ a meticulous 10-day seed separation process. The seeds actually enhance the product's taste, creating natural variety in flavor profiles. We save leftover pulp to create delicious passion fruit and pineapple jam.

Production Specifications

  • Machine operates maximum 3 hours per session
  • 2 production cycles possible per day
  • Shorter processing time yields better taste
  • pH water used exclusively for mixing
