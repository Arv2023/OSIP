About Malino Passion Fruit Syrup

Malino Passion Fruit Syrup is a family-owned business dedicated to creating premium, all-natural passion fruit products. Our journey began with a commitment to quality and authenticity, rebranding from our family's previous venture to eliminate artificial ingredients and focus on pure, natural flavors.

Our Business

Small Team, Big Quality

Our operation consists of just 2 dedicated employees, with the owner personally handling all packaging to ensure every product meets our high-quality standards.

Business Model

We operate as a wholesale supplier - customers purchase from us and resell to their own markets. No retail sales directly to end consumers.

Pricing

We offer 2 bottles for $10, keeping our prices affordable so our buyers can maintain healthy profit margins when reselling.

Special Offer: New customers pay only half price on their first purchase!

Our Product

100% Natural Ingredients

  • 85% Premium Passion Fruit - sourced from trusted suppliers
  • 15% Pure Sugar and pH Water - no regular water used
  • No artificial ingredients, colors, or citrus
  • All Natural - promoting authentic tropical flavor

Quality Distinction

Our authentic product maintains the natural passion fruit color, distinguishing it from competitors who use artificial coloring that creates an unnatural orange hue. Our commitment to natural ingredients ensures superior taste and quality.

Freshness Guarantee

Shelf Life: 12 months from production date

Stock Rotation: We maintain 3-month stock cycles, restocking every 3 months to ensure freshness

Our Production Process

Artisanal Manufacturing

Each batch is carefully crafted using our specialized equipment that processes 100kg of passion fruit per production cycle. The entire process takes considerable time to ensure optimal flavor extraction and quality.

Seed Separation Process

We employ a meticulous 10-day seed separation process. The seeds actually enhance the product's taste, creating natural variety in flavor profiles. We save leftover pulp to create delicious passion fruit and pineapple jam.

Production Specifications

  • Machine operates maximum 3 hours per session
  • 2 production cycles possible per day
  • Shorter processing time yields better taste
  • pH water used exclusively for mixing

Distribution & Ordering

Current Distribution

We currently distribute our products to bread stores near the airport and are actively expanding to more retail locations.

How to Order

Pre-order Required: All purchases must be pre-ordered by calling our contact number. We're exploring online ordering options with delivery services.

Contact information is provided on product boxes for easy reordering.

Equipment Upgrades

Current Challenges & Solutions

Operational Challenges

  • Limited Equipment: Single production machine limits daily output capacity
  • Power Dependency: Frequent power outages halt production
  • Maintenance: Equipment repairs require long trips to Makassar, increasing costs
  • Space Constraints: Limited facility space restricts expansion opportunities

Market Challenges

  • Customer Acquisition: Difficulty reaching new customers despite having loyal regular buyers
  • Marketing: Limited advertising and promotional activities
  • Competition: Competing against artificial products with lower production costs

Sustainability & Waste Management

Currently, passion fruit skins are discarded as waste. We're exploring opportunities to convert this waste into useful byproducts, aligning with sustainable business practices.
