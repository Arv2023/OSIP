About Malino Passion Fruit Syrup

A family business producing premium passion fruit syrup that captures the authentic taste of tropical paradise

Our Story

Origins

Our journey began 2 years ago when our family started this business while working other jobs. We started with a simple mixer and have grown steadily since then.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, all-natural passion fruit syrup that brings authentic tropical flavor to homes and businesses.

Pricing Strategy

We offer 2 bottles for $10, keeping our prices affordable so our buyers can maintain healthy profit margins when reselling.

Special Offer: New customers pay only half price on their first purchase!

Our Product

100% Natural Ingredients

  • 85% Premium Passion Fruit - sourced from trusted suppliers
  • 15% Pure Sugar and pH Water - no regular water used
  • No artificial ingredients, colors, or citrus
  • All Natural - promoting authentic tropical flavor

Quality Distinction

We don't add citrus or artificial colors. We use passion fruit sourced from trusted suppliers who provide high-quality fruits.

Our production process involves heating the passion fruit with sugar and pure water - no regular tap water used.

Product Varieties

  • Classic Passion Fruit Syrup (our signature product)
  • Passion Fruit-Pineapple Jam (available upon request)

Packaging & Sizes

We use clear bottles to showcase the vibrant color of our syrup. Our bottles are easy to handle and store.

Production Process

Current Capacity

We can produce approximately 50 bottles per production run using our current equipment. Our simple production process allows us to maintain quality while gradually scaling up.

Equipment

We use a basic mixer for production. While simple, it allows us to maintain the quality and authenticity of our product.

Quality Control

Every batch is carefully monitored to ensure consistent taste and quality. We take pride in our hands-on approach to production.

Distribution & Marketing

Primary Distribution Channel

We primarily sell through direct channels - personal networks and word-of-mouth referrals. This allows us to build strong relationships with our customers.

Future Distribution Plans

We're exploring online ordering options with delivery services.

Contact information is provided on product boxes for easy reordering.

Sampling & Events

We offer free samples for testing and regularly participate in exhibitions and showcase events to introduce our products to new customers.

Future Development

Product Expansion

Planning to develop ready-to-drink syrup (just add water) based on customer requests and introduce more product varieties including our passion fruit-pineapple jam.

Equipment Upgrades

Looking to invest in larger mixing machines and improved filtration systems to increase production efficiency and meet growing demand.

Collaboration Opportunities

Open to partnerships with coffee shops, restaurants, and retail stores to expand our reach.

Challenges We Face

Current Obstacles

  • Seasonal Fruit Supply: Passion fruit availability varies by season, affecting our production schedule
  • Limited Equipment: Our current mixer capacity limits production volume
  • Market Awareness: Building brand recognition in a competitive market
  • Competition: Competing against artificial products with lower production costs

Sustainability & Waste Management

Currently, passion fruit skins are discarded as waste. We're exploring opportunities to convert this waste into useful byproducts, aligning with sustainable business practices.
