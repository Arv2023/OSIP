About Makissar Passion Fruit Syrup

Makissar Passion Fruit Syrup is a family-owned business dedicated to creating premium, all-natural passion fruit products. Our journey began with a commitment to quality and authenticity, rebranding from our family's previous venture to eliminate artificial ingredients and focus on pure, natural flavors.

Our Production Process

Artisanal Manufacturing

Each batch is carefully crafted using our specialized equipment that processes 100kg of passion fruit per production cycle. The entire process takes considerable time to ensure optimal flavor extraction and quality.

Seed Separation Process

We employ a meticulous 10-day seed separation process. The seeds actually enhance the product's taste, creating natural variety in flavor profiles. We save leftover pulp to create delicious passion fruit and pineapple jam.

Production Specifications

  • Machine operates maximum 3 hours per session
  • 2 production cycles possible per day
  • Shorter processing time yields better taste
  • pH water used exclusively for mixing

Distribution & Ordering

Current Distribution

We currently distribute our products to bread stores near the airport and are actively expanding to more retail locations.

We offer free samples for testing and regularly participate in exhibitions and showcase events to introduce our products to new customers.

Our Future Plans

Product Expansion

Planning to develop ready-to-drink syrup (just add water) based on customer requests and introduce more product varieties including our passion fruit-pineapple jam.

Equipment Upgrades

Looking to invest in larger mixing machines and improved filtration systems to increase production efficiency and meet growing demand.

Open to partnerships with collaborators to learn new machine operations and develop innovative products.
